Healthy Living Hub - Project Site Plan

"Healthy Living Hub" is a mini-resource center for people looking to lead a healthier lifestyle.

Site Name

Healthy Living Hub

This name represents a website focused on promoting a healthy lifestyle by offering resources on nutrition, fitness, and mental well-being. The name was selected to reflect the holistic approach to health and create an inviting space for users seeking improvement in various aspects of their well-being.

Site Purpose

The Healthy Living Hub aims to provide comprehensive information on living a healthier lifestyle. Users will find healthy daily meal plans, daily exercise routines, and tips for mental well-being. The site will also include interactive tools like a calorie calculator. Visitors will be encouraged to return frequently to utilize dynamic tools and discover new content.

Scenarios

  • How can I create a personalized meal plan based on my fitness goals?
  • What are the best exercises for beginners looking to improve their fitness level?
  • How can I calculate calories?

Logo

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (Hex: #6ba292): Used for the header background, headings, and primary buttons.
  • Accent Color (Hex: #d2691e): Used for underlining headings, links, and call-to-action buttons.
  • Background Color (Hex: #f5f5f5): Light background used for the overall page to maintain a clean and calm feel.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif – used for body text and paragraphs for clear readability.
  • Secondary Font: Georgia, serif – used for headings to give them a distinctive and elegant look.
